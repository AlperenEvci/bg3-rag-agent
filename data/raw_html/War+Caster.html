                War Caster

                You gain Advantage on Saving Throws to maintain Concentration on a spell.

                You can also use a reaction to cast Shocking Grasp at a target moving out of melee range.

                War Caster is a Feat in Baldur's Gate 3. Feats are special talents or expertises that provide special capabilities to characters. At certain levels, your character will get to choose between an Ability Score Improvement Feature or a Feat. (Note this feat is not available in EA and all info is subject to change.)

                 

                War Caster Information

                • You gain Advantage on Saving Throws to maintain Concentration on a spell.
                • You can also use a reaction to cast Shocking Grasp at a target moving out of melee range.
                • Class Feature
                  • War Caster: Concentration
                  • War Caster: Opportunity Spell

                 

                How to unlock War Caster

                You'll need to fulfill the following requirements to unlock War Caster

                • Spellcast ability
                • All Classes at Level 4

                 

                War Caster Tips & Notes

                • Notes & Tips go here
                • Tested on Warlock on the latest patch - v4.1.1.3696083, Shocking Grasp does seem to scale with your spellcasting ability, the tested ability was CHA.
                • The CON Save Proficiency stacks with Advantage.
                • By getting this feat, you can choose between Shocking Grasp or Normal Melee while receiving Shocking Grasp that can only be used as a Reaction during your turn.
