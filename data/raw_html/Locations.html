                Locations | Baldurs Gate 3 Wiki


                Locations in Baldur's Gate 3 covers the different areas that can be explored in the game. Players can encounter and discover various Enemies, Bosses, NPCs, types of equipment, and much more for each location. This page covers a list of the various areas that can be discovered in the game.

                Digging

                Players are now able to dig certain places to find different types of loot. In order to do so, players need to use a Shovel. Sometimes, there will be obvious hints on where to dig, but others players may need to learn about digging places by reading or hearing different conversations.
