                Distracting Strike

                Effect

                Distract your target, giving your allies Advantage on their next Attack Roll against the target.

                Requirements

                Lvl 3 Battle Master (Fighter Subclass)
                Martial Adept (Feat) grants this feature

                Distracting Strike is a Passive Feature in Baldur's Gate 3. Distracting Strike grants a simple way to give your allies Advantage against the distracted enemy. Features provide unique abilities that are inherent to a Character's Race, Class, Background selected. These may vary greatly, from allowing you to equip certain Weapons or pieces of Armor with Proficiency, to add your Proficiency Bonus to certain Saving Throws.

                 

                Distracting Strike Information

                Distract your target, giving your allies Advantage on their next Attack Roll against the target.

                 

                How to unlock Distracting Strike

                Distracting Strike can be acquired by:

                • Lvl 3 Battle Master (Fighter Subclass)
                • Martial Adept (Feat) grants this feature.
