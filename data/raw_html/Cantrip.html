                Cantrip | Baldurs Gate 3 Wiki


                Cantrip Spells in Baldur's Gate 3 covers the most basic and easy spells Characters can use. Cantrip Spells can be acquired through Features, Classes and many other ways. Damaging Cantrips increase in damage at Levels 5 and 10, allowing them to be more effective as the game progresses. Note that the damage these Cantrips deal is based on Character Level, not Class Level, allowing characters who use Cantrips to Multiclass and not lose the effectiveness of these Spells.
