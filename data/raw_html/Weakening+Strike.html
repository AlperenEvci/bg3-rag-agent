                Weakening Strike | Baldurs Gate 3 Wiki


                Weakening Strike

                Weapon Actions

                5~8 Damage

                1d4+4Slashing

                This non-lethal attack possibly inflicts Weak Grip, hindering the target's attacks.

                Creatures without weapons can't have Weak Grip.

                2 turns

                Melee STR Save  Attack Roll Short Rest
                 Action  

                Weakening Strike is an Action in Baldur's Gate 3. Actions can be used in both exploration and combat to maneuver across the battlefield or to harm or aid Characters. The actions that a Character can perform are based on their Class and Equipment.

                 

                Weakening Strike Information

                • This non-lethal attack possibly inflicts Weak Grip, hindering the target's attacks. Creatures without weapons can't have Weak Grip.
                • 1d4+4Slashing
                • 2 turns
                • Type:  Action
                • Melee

                 

                How to unlock Weakening Strike

                Weakening Strike can be unlocked by the following types of Weapons

                • Rapiers
                • War Picks
                • Most Warhammers:
                  • Intransigent Warhammer
                  • Faithbreaker
                  • Warhammer +1
                  • Warhammer +2
