                Shortswords | Baldurs Gate 3 Wiki


                Shortswords in Baldur's Gate 3 are pieces of equipment that is used to inflict damage against hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses. Shortswords are Light, Finesse, Martial Weapons in BG3 that can be one-handed, dealing 1-6 Damage without enchantments. They have a range of 1.5m, and they deal Piercing Damage, though some special Shortswords also deal another type of Damage as well. The exception to this is the Practice Sword that deals 1-4 Bludgeoning damage instead.

                Many Shortswords have unique properties, so you should read carefully what these do, and many also have Weapon Actions that can only be used if you are Proficient with Shortswords in BG3. These Weapon Actions are Flourish and Piercing Strike.
