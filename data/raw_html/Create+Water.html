                Create or Destroy Water

                Level 1 Transmutation Spell

                Call forth rain or destroy a water-based.

                9m
                 Action Level 1 Spell Slot  

                Create or Destroy Water is a Spell in Baldur's Gate 3. Create or Destroy Water is a Lvl 1 Spell from the Transmutation school. Spells can be used for dealing damage to Enemies, inflict Status Ailments, buff Characters or interact with the environment.

                 

                Baldur's Gate 3 Create or Destroy Water Information

                • Description: Call forth rain or destroy a water-based.
                • Level: Lvl 1 Spell
                • School: Transmutation
                • Casting Time: Action
                • Range: 9m 4m
                • Requires Concentration: No
                • Saving Throw: None

                 

                How to Acquire Create or Destroy Water in BG 3

                • Create or Destroy Water can be acquired by the following classes:
                  • Druid, Cleric, Storm Sorcery (Lvl 6 Sorcerer Subclass)
                • Create or Destroy Water can be cast by using the following Items:
                  • Rain Dancer

                 

                Create or Destroy Water Tips & Notes for BG 3

                • Upcast: When cast with a 2nd level spell slot or higher, the radius increases by 2m for each slot level above 1st. 
                • Create water applies the Wet condition: Vulnerability to Cold and Lightning damage (double damage).
                • Lightning Spells cast on water causes the Shocked condition: Removing their reaction and having a chance to stun. 
                • Electrified Water + a Fire Spell causes Electrified Steam, which resets the Shocked and Wet conditions.
                • A Cold spell freezes the water/mist and creates an Ice Surface, causing Difficult Terrain (half Movement Speed) and may cause creatures in the area to fall Prone unless succeeding a DEX Save.
                • E.g. Create Water + Glyph of Warding combo.
