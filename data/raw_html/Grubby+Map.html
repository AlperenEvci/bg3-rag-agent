                Grubby Map

                Books and Lore
                ACTION

                A crudely drawn map marred by annotations and personal notes.

                Weight 0.05 Value 14

                Grubby Map is an Item that belongs in Books and Lore in Baldur's Gate 3. Some Items can be consumed granting various effects like buffing the Character or restoring HP, while others can be used to interact with the environment or provide Lore about Baldur's Gate 3 world.

                 

                A crudely drawn map marred by annotations and personal notes. 

                Grubby Map Information

                "[On one side of this page, a hand-drawn map depicts a northern grotto bordering the Ebonlake. An attacker's account marks the other.]

                One of the drugning shrooms sensed us and split. Chunky one, too. Month's worth of soup, at least. The rest were too busy droning to put up a fuss.

                Nere pays us, and I'm bolting for Mantol-Derith, Thrinn's orders be damned. They got eelsteak, mineral mead, and top-land food of all sort. Clan can't live on fungus alone."

                • Weight 0.05
                • Value 14

                 

                Grubby Map Location & Where to Find

                • Location: can be found at Grymforge. Once you reach Grymforge by boat, will find two Duergar there. Just near you, you can find the Grubby Map and Battered Note. 
                • Quest: ??
