                Find Familiar

                Level 1 Conjuration Spell

                Summon a familiar, a fey spirit that takes an animal form of your choosing.

                18m
                 Action Level 1 Spell Slot  

                Find Familiar is a Spell in Baldur's Gate 3. Find Familiar is a Lvl 1 Spell from the Conjuration school. Spells can be used for dealing damage to Enemies, inflict Status Ailments, buff Characters or interact with the environment.

                Find Familiar Information

                • Description: Gain the service of a familiar, a fey spirit that takes an animal form of your choosing:
                  • Find Familiar: Spider Summon spider familiar. Its deadly bite can poison enemies. 
                  • Find Familiar: Raven Summon a raven familiar. It can launch precise attacks.
                  • Find Familiar: Frog Summon a frog familiar. Its skin is covered with dizzying toxins.
                  • Find Familiar: Cat Summon a cat familiar. Its meow can distract your enemies.
                  • Find Familiar: Crab Summon a crab familiar. Its sharp pincers can slow enemies.
                  • Find Familiar: Rat Summon a rat familiar. Its infectious bite can spread disease.
                  • Find Familiar: Scratch Any character with Scratch's Ball can summon Scratch. Scratch will automatically be dismissed if his summoner returns to camp.
                • Level: Lvl 1 spell
                • School: Conjuration School
                • Casting Time: Action
                • Range: 18m
                • Requires Concentration: No
                • Saving Throw: None
                • Ritual Spell: Yes

                How to Acquire Find Familiar

                Find Familiar can be acquired by the following classes:

                • Wizard
                • Ranger
                • Eldritch Knight (Fighter)
                • Arcane Trickster (Rogue)
                • Warlock Pact of the Chain Pact Boon

                Find Familiar can be cast by using the following Items:

                • Scroll of Summon Quasit

                 

                Find Familiar Tips & Notes

                • Any character with Scratch's Ball can summon Scratch. Scratch will automatically be dismissed if his summoner returns to camp.
                • Find Familiar is a ritual spell which can be cast without expending a spell slot outside combat.
                • Rangers, at creation (level 1), can choose one of the five available Natural Explorer features called Beast Tamer.
                • An invisible familiar can safely be placed next to an enemy to give the caster Advantage on Attack Rolls against it.
